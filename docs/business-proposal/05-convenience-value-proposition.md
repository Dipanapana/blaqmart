## The Traditional Back-to-School Experience

### What Parents Currently Go Through

**Step 1: Get the Stationery List**
- Wait for school to send list (often late)
- Decipher unclear requirements
- Research which brands are acceptable

**Step 2: Plan the Shopping Trip**
- Take time off work
- Arrange transport
- Find someone to watch younger kids
- Budget for fuel and food

**Step 3: The Shopping Marathon**
- Drive to town (30-60 minutes each way)
- Queue at multiple stores
- Search for items across aisles
- Deal with stock shortages
- Make compromises on brands
- Carry heavy bags
- Queue again to pay

**Step 4: The Return Trip(s)**
- Realize you forgot something
- Make another trip
- Repeat the whole process
