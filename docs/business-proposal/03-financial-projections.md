# Financial Projections - Blaqmart Stationery

## Revenue Model

### Primary Revenue Streams

1. **Individual Product Sales** (40% of revenue)
   - Parents buying specific items
   - Average order: R150
   - Margin: 50-100%

2. **Stationery Pack Sales** (50% of revenue)
   - Complete grade bundles
   - Average order: R450
   - Margin: 40-60%

3. **Custom Hamper Builder** (10% of revenue)
   - Parents selecting items for gift baskets
   - Average order: R300
   - Margin: 60-80%

## Break-Even Analysis

### Fixed Costs per Month
- Hosting: R500
- Marketing: R1,000
- Fuel Base: R1,000
- Misc: R500
- **Total: R3,000/month**

### Variable Costs per Order
- COGS: 50%
- Processing: 2.6%
- Packaging: R10
- Fuel: R40

### Break-Even Calculation

```
Average Order Value: R450
Contribution Margin: R450 - R225 (COGS) - R12 (processing) - R50 (delivery/pack)
                   = R163 per order

Break-Even Orders: R3,000 / R163 = 18.4 orders/month
```

**We break even at just 19 orders per month!**

## Conclusion

Blaqmart Stationery presents a financially sound business opportunity with:

- **Low startup costs:** R36,700 fully invested
- **Quick break-even:** 19 orders/month
- **Strong margins:** 50% gross, 31% net
- **Fast payback:** Investment recovered in 6 months
- **Scalable model:** Room for 10x growth in 3 years

The combination of direct sourcing, no physical storefront, and efficient local delivery creates a cost structure that allows for competitive pricing while maintaining healthy profitability.
