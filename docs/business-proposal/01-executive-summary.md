# Blaqmart Stationery - Executive Summary

## Business Overview

**Company Name:** Blaqmart Stationery
**Location:** Warrenton, Northern Cape, South Africa
**Launch Date:** Mid-January 2025
**Website:** blaqmart.co.za

### Mission Statement
To provide families in the Northern Cape with affordable, quality school stationery through a convenient online shopping experience, eliminating the stress and high costs of back-to-school shopping.

---

## The Problem We Solve

### Current Pain Points for Parents

1. **High Prices at Local Retailers**
   - CNA, PEP, and local shops charge premium prices
   - Parents pay 20-50% more than necessary
   - No bulk discounts available for families

2. **Inconvenient Shopping Experience**
   - Long queues during back-to-school rush
   - Limited stock and selection
   - Multiple trips to find all items on school lists
   - Travel costs to Kimberley for better selection

3. **Confusion About Requirements**
   - Different schools have different stationery lists
   - Parents unsure which products are appropriate for each grade
   - Time wasted figuring out what to buy

---

## Our Solution

### Grade-Specific Shopping
We organize products by grade (Grade R to Matric), so parents only see what their child actually needs.

### Pre-Made Stationery Packs
Complete stationery bundles for each grade level:
- **Grade R Pack:** R199.99 (saves R60)
- **Grade 1 Pack:** R299.99 (saves R100)
- **Grade 4 Pack:** R449.99 (saves R130)
- **Grade 8 Pack:** R699.99 (saves R200)
- **Matric Pack:** R849.99 (saves R250)

### Local Delivery with Cash on Delivery
- **Free same-day delivery** in Warrenton & Jan Kempdorp
- **Cash on Delivery** for customers who prefer not to pay online
- No need to drive anywhere
