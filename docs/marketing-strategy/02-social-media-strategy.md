# Social Media Strategy - Blaqmart Stationery

## Platform Strategy

### Primary Platform: Facebook

**Why Facebook:**
- 95%+ of target audience uses Facebook
- Local community groups are active
- Marketplace integration
- Cost-effective advertising
- Older demographic comfortable here

**Page Setup:**
- Page Name: Blaqmart Stationery
- Category: E-commerce / School Supplies
- Location: Warrenton, Northern Cape
- Call-to-Action: Shop Now → blaqmart.co.za

### Secondary Platform: WhatsApp

**Why WhatsApp:**
- Universal in South Africa
- Direct, personal communication
- Free to use
- Order confirmations
- Customer support

**Setup:**
- WhatsApp Business account
- Quick Replies configured
- Catalog linked to products
- Business hours set
- Auto-greeting message

### Tertiary Platform: Instagram (Future)

**When to Launch:** After 500+ Facebook followers
**Focus:** Visual product showcases, happy customer photos

## Facebook Advertising Strategy

### Budget: R6,000/year (R500/month average)

### Campaign Structure

#### Campaign 1: Awareness (December)
**Objective:** Reach
**Budget:** R1,500
**Audience:**
- Location: 50km radius of Warrenton
- Age: 25-55
- Interests: Parenting, Education, Shopping
**Creative:** Video introducing Blaqmart

#### Campaign 2: Consideration (January Week 1-2)
**Objective:** Traffic
**Budget:** R1,500
**Audience:**
- Location: Warrenton, Jan Kempdorp, Hartswater, Christiana
- Age: 28-50
- Interests: School, Children, Education
**Creative:** Carousel of grade packs

#### Campaign 3: Conversion (January Week 3-4)
**Objective:** Conversions
**Budget:** R2,000
**Audience:**
- Website visitors
- Engaged with Facebook page
- Lookalike of purchasers
**Creative:** "Last chance" messaging with discounts

#### Campaign 4: Retargeting (Feb-Nov)
**Objective:** Conversions
**Budget:** R1,000
**Audience:**
- Cart abandoners
- Past purchasers
- Page engagers
**Creative:** Top-up reminders, new products

### Ad Creative Guidelines

**Images:**
- Bright, colorful product photos
- Happy children with stationery
- Organized pack contents
- Before/after comparisons
- Price comparison graphics

**Copy:**
- Lead with benefit (save money/time)
- Include price
- Clear call-to-action
- Mention free delivery/COD
- Keep under 125 characters for main text

**Video:**
- 15-30 seconds max
- Captions required (many watch muted)
- Start with hook in first 3 seconds
- Show product in use
- End with clear CTA

## Crisis Management

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Negative Review:**
- Respond within 2 hours
- Apologize publicly, solve privately
- Offer solution/compensation
- Follow up to change rating

**Delivery Complaint:**
- Acknowledge immediately
- Investigate same day
- Offer resolution + goodwill gesture
- Post resolved outcome (with permission)

**Stock Shortage:**
- Communicate proactively
- Offer alternatives or refund
- Update estimated availability
- Thank for patience

## Summary

Blaqmart's social media strategy focuses on:

1. **Facebook as primary platform** - where our audience lives
2. **WhatsApp for direct sales** - personal, trusted, SA-preferred
3. **Value-driven content** - education, savings, convenience
4. **Local community focus** - building trust and connection
5. **Paid ads in peak season** - maximize January opportunity
6. **Consistent engagement** - responsive, helpful, human

With a R6,000 annual ad budget and consistent organic posting, we can reach 3,000+ local families and convert 500+ to customers in Year 1.
