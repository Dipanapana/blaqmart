## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone
- Friendly and warm
- Helpful, not pushy
- Local and relatable
- Professional but not corporate
- Understanding of parent struggles

### Language
- Simple, clear Afrikaans/English
- Avoid jargon
- Use "we" and "you"
- Empathize with challenges
- Celebrate wins with customers

### Emojis
Use sparingly and appropriately:
- ✅ For lists/checkmarks
- 💰 For savings/money
- 🚚 For delivery
- 📚 For stationery/education
- ⏰ For urgency
- 🙏 For thank you
- ⭐ For reviews

### Don'ts
- Don't be pushy or aggressive
- Don't use ALL CAPS excessively
- Don't make false claims
- Don't badmouth competitors by name
- Don't ignore complaints
- Don't take too long to respond
