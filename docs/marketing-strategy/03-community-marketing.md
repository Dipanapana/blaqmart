# Community Marketing Strategy - Blaqmart Stationery

## Why Community Marketing Matters

In small towns like Warrenton and Jan Kempdorp, trust is everything. People buy from people they know. Community marketing leverages this by:

- Building personal relationships
- Earning word-of-mouth referrals
- Creating local brand ambassadors
- Establishing credibility through association

**Cost:** Mostly time, minimal budget
**Impact:** Highest trust, longest-lasting

## School Partnership Program

### Overview
Partner with local schools to become their recommended stationery supplier.

### Target Schools

| School | Town | Grades | Est. Students | Priority |
|--------|------|--------|---------------|----------|
| Warrenton Primary | Warrenton | R-7 | 800 | High |
| Warrenton High | Warrenton | 8-12 | 600 | High |
| Jan Kempdorp Primary | Jan Kempdorp | R-7 | 600 | High |
| Floors Combined | Warrenton | R-9 | 400 | Medium |
| Hartswater Primary | Hartswater | R-7 | 500 | Medium |
| Christiana Primary | Christiana | R-7 | 450 | Medium |

### Partnership Proposal

**What We Offer Schools:**

1. **Customized Packs per Grade**
   - Aligned exactly to school's stationery list
   - Updated annually with school input
   - Consistent quality guaranteed

2. **Fundraising Opportunity**
   - 5% of sales donated to school
   - Or R10 per pack sold
   - Can fund specific projects

3. **Convenience for Parents**
   - One link to order everything
   - No guessing or mistakes
   - Delivered before school starts

4. **Marketing Support**
   - Co-branded flyers for distribution
   - Digital graphics for school Facebook
   - SMS/WhatsApp message templates

**What We Need from Schools:**

1. Current stationery list per grade
2. Permission to use school name
3. Distribution of flyers to parents
4. Optional: Mention in school communications

### Approach Strategy

**Step 1: Research** (November)
- Collect current stationery lists
- Identify decision-makers (Principal, SGB)
- Understand school's fundraising needs

**Step 2: Initial Contact** (November-December)
- Formal letter to Principal
- Include partnership proposal document
- Sample pack for inspection

**Step 3: Meeting** (December)
- Present benefits in person
- Address concerns
- Agree on terms

**Step 4: Implementation** (December-January)
- Create school-specific packs
- Co-brand materials
- Coordinate distribution

## Word-of-Mouth & Referral Program

### The Power of Referrals
- Referrals convert 4x better than cold traffic
- Customer acquisition cost near R0
- Built-in trust factor
- Creates community buzz

### Referral Program Structure

**For the Referrer (Existing Customer):**
- R50 credit for each successful referral
- Credit applied to next order
- No limit on referrals
- Tracked via unique code

**For the Referred (New Customer):**
- 10% off first order
- Free delivery (if not already free)
- Welcome gift (branded pencil)

### How It Works

1. Customer makes purchase
2. Receives unique referral code (e.g., THABO50)
3. Shares code with friends/family
4. Friend uses code at checkout → gets 10% off
5. Original customer gets R50 credit

### Referral Promotion

```
💰 EARN R50 FOR EVERY FRIEND!

Love Blaqmart? Share the savings!

1. Use your unique code: [CODE]
2. Share with friends
3. When they order, they get 10% off
4. YOU get R50 credit!

No limits. Share away!

Find your code in your order confirmation
or WhatsApp us at 072 123 4567.
```

### Tracking Referrals
- Unique codes per customer
- Track in order notes
- Monthly referral leaderboard
- Top referrers get bonus prizes

## WhatsApp Community Building

### WhatsApp Groups Strategy

**DO NOT:** Create your own group (seen as spam)

**DO:** Get added to existing community groups

### Approach for Group Entry

1. Identify active community WhatsApp groups
2. Connect with group admins personally
3. Offer value (not just advertising)
4. Request permission to post occasionally
5. Share genuinely helpful content

### Content for Groups

**Acceptable:**
- Answering stationery questions
- Sharing back-to-school tips
- Occasional promotional post (with admin approval)
- Helpful information

**Not Acceptable:**
- Spamming promotions
- Posting without permission
- Being pushy or salesy
- Ignoring group rules

### Sample Group Post (With Permission)

```
Hi everyone! 👋

Quick reminder that schools open in 10 days.

If anyone needs stationery, I run Blaqmart - we deliver
free in Warrenton and Jan Kempdorp.

Our packs are 20-40% cheaper than the shops.

Check out blaqmart.co.za or WhatsApp me at 072 123 4567.

(Posted with admin permission. Thanks [Admin Name]!)
```

## Summary

Community marketing is Blaqmart's secret weapon. By building genuine relationships with schools, churches, and local organizations, we create a trusted network that drives sustainable word-of-mouth growth.

**Key Activities:**
1. School partnerships for direct access to parents
2. Church announcements for trust-building
3. Referral program for customer-driven growth
4. Local events for visibility
5. Testimonials for social proof

**Investment:** Mostly time, ~R3,000 in materials
**Return:** Highest trust, lowest acquisition cost, long-term loyalty
