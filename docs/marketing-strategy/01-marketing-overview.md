## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Brand Awareness
**Mitigation:** Heavy January push, school partnerships, word-of-mouth incentives

### Risk 2: Trust Issues (New Business)
**Mitigation:** COD option, local presence, testimonials, money-back guarantee

### Risk 3: Limited Marketing Budget
**Mitigation:** Focus on free/low-cost channels (WhatsApp, word-of-mouth, organic social)

### Risk 4: Competitor Response
**Mitigation:** Lock in school partnerships, build customer loyalty, maintain price advantage
