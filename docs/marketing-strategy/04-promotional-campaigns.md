## A/B Testing Plan

### What to Test

**Subject Lines (Email/WhatsApp):**
- A: "Don't miss out! School stationery on sale"
- B: "Save R200 on your child's stationery"

**Call-to-Action:**
- A: "Shop Now"
- B: "Order Today"
- C: "Get Your Pack"

**Discount Types:**
- A: Percentage off (10% off)
- B: Rand amount (Save R50)
- C: Free gift (Free pencil case)

**Urgency Messaging:**
- A: Time-based ("Ends Sunday")
- B: Stock-based ("Only 10 left")
- C: Scarcity ("First 20 orders only")

### How to Test
- Split WhatsApp broadcast 50/50
- Run parallel Facebook ads
- Track conversion rates per variation
- Apply learnings to next campaign

## Emergency Promotions

### Stock Clearance
**When:** Overstocked items
**Offer:** Up to 50% off specific items
**Duration:** Until sold out

### Slow Day Boost
**When:** Weekday with low orders
**Offer:** Free express delivery today only
**Duration:** Single day

### Competitor Response
**When:** Competitor runs sale
**Offer:** Price match + 5% extra
**Duration:** Match competitor duration

## Summary

Blaqmart's promotional strategy focuses on:

1. **Seasonal Peaks:** Heavy promotion during back-to-school (Dec-Jan)
2. **Urgency Creation:** Countdown messaging, limited offers
3. **Customer Lifecycle:** Different offers for new vs. returning customers
4. **Testing & Learning:** Continuous optimization based on results
5. **Always-On Programs:** Referrals, teacher discounts, bulk pricing

With R6,000+ in planned promotional spend, we aim to generate 500+ orders in Year 1 with an average acquisition cost under R15 per order.
